Compound Machines System

Project: 1.1.6 Compound Machines
Course: Honors Principles of Engineering
Duration: Approximately 2.5 weeks
Objective: Design, build, test and manufacture a product that can help make the lives of the people easier in a day to day circumstance by creating a machine to solve one such task. The machine must have four simple machines.
Type of Project: Group Project

-

The objective is to create a product with VEX parts that will use four simple machines to complete a daily task. With my prior experience with VEX parts, I knew how the parts would fit together, as well as how to use tools to move parts. Thus, after a teammate gave an idea, I helped take that idea into the design stage. My team decided that my drawing skills out of the team of four was the neatest and most understandable, so this combined with my VEX skills, allowed me to create the strongest design sketch possible. I was able to draw a sketch to take an outlined idea into a more solid plan to a reality. I was not team manager, since I was focused on driving the build team. Thus, I did start the documentation or create the document. This meant I was not team manager by the rules, but I was leading my team through the building process. I was instructing my teammates how to help with the build, with their input. We worked together as a team, giving each other feedback between the build and documentation parts of our teams, exemplifying cooperation. Our design moved a gate up, without human contact which is especially important during this pandemic.

GenAI Lit Review

Project: Generative Artificial Intelligence - Literature Review
Course: AP Computer Science Applications
Duration: April 14 - April 22 2024
Objective: Objective: Write a literature review on a use of generative AI
Type of Project: Individual Project

-

My paper focused on how Learning Curves and Misconceptions are magnified and effected by Generative AI, including both the positive and negative impacts in the education and academic world. Generative AI, such as GenAI, synthesizes responses to queries by predicting word sequences based on extensive web data. While it has the capability to generate highly realistic fake news, posing serious threats to the integrity of information, it also offers substantial benefits in educational settings. Students can leverage AI to enhance their learning, although there is a risk that it might impede their learning progress by creating dependencies. This underscores the need for a balanced approach in using AI to provide personalized learning experiences while guarding against the dissemination of inaccurate information and excessive reliance on technological assistance.
